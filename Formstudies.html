The unique character of many objects in our contemporary world are disappearing day by day. Crafts-manship is shifted to the background with the upcoming of mass production, who takes the lead in our economy. Meraki shows our love for arts and crafts. Multiple experiments with different natural materials lead us to using wood for our project.


The three sculptures shown above, show different nuances of the used material for Meraki. Each figure has its own features and differentiates itself from others trough endless fractures. In addition, the images each have their own unique process of hammer and chisel tracks and thus obtain their own unique identity.
