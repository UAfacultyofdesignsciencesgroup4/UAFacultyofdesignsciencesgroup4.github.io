The two dimensional work represents the infinity of Meraki. The creation of a shadow, by illuminating an object from different perspective, ensures a unique craft with a lot of possibilities. Each shadow can lead to a new notable object. Meraki differentiates itself from other projects by its infinite presence.

The three dimensional volume is formed by combining three characteristic shadows. The prototype exists of three shadows with each its own visible structures, these are assimilated in the texture of the material. All these different items combines come together in a sculptural object, which formes a bridge between craft, art and user.
