For photographing the end result, we returned to the origin of the product. We have therefore chosen the location where it all started for us, namely sawmill Peleman in Puurs. A location where craft and nature come together in a harmonious way. Briefly, the perfect location where our project started and came to his end.

Following the previous series of photographs, we opted for an artisanal environment that matched the texture of our design. This took place at Axel Vervoordt, a great source of inspiration for both our design and prototype. His way of material engineering, natural atmosphere and sense of unity appealed to us to take part in our own process.

PreviousNext
We also felt it was crucial to photograph our prototype in a neutral space so that our design would come into its own. For this we chose the ‘wintertuin’ because of its sober and understated atmosphere. In this way the structure and color nuances of the material became clearly visible.
